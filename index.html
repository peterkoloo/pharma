Welcome to Balcure Pharmaceuticals Limited. We are a leading Importer, Distributor and Exporter of Pharmaceuticals, Medical Consumables and Medical Equipment. We are based in Nairobi Kenya, the heart of East Africa and trade across East Africa.

Our Passion to Customer Service

We are always on the look out to provide our customers with quality healthcare products and solutions at the most competitive prices. We market a wide range of customized pharmaceuticals and non-pharmaceutical products with a comprehensive range of specialty for treatment of patients. We clearly understand our customer’s needs and use a cutting- edge technology to present innovative solutions to them. Our business includes Branded- Generics in East Africa. In Kenya, we have posted high growth specialty segment of Hematinic, Antimalarials Antibacterials, Cough Expectorant & Antitussives and Analgesics & Antipyretics. We pride ourselves on excellent levels of customer satisfaction and distribution services.

Our Clients Include:

Community Pharmacy

Community pharmacists are the health professionals mostly accessible to the public across the globe. Today, an increasingly wide range of new and analogous pharmaceuticals are used in medicine and Balcure Pharmaceuticals Limited is committed to ensure availability of specialized pharmaceuticals in addition to essential medicines.

Hospital Pharmacy

Hospital Pharmacy is responsible for the provision of pharmaceuticals to in-patient and out-patient as a department within the hospital. In addition to supplying Pharmaceuticals, our commitment goes beyond supplying but also to training and educating these pharmacists about the drug information, patient counseling, medicines compounding and distribution and we achieve this through our numerous organized and facilitated CMEs.

Healthcare Institution

Healthcare Institutions play vital role in diagnosis and treatment of illnesses and injuries and we come in by providing them with all the necessary support that ensures success in their daily routine services to the patients.

Doctors

Beyond medical school and housemanship, each doctor need to keep themselves with the current changes in pharmaceutical industry. Balcure Pharmaceuticals Limited organizes CMEs to ensure that doctors practice up-to-date medicine and offer good quality patient care.

Accreditation

We hold Agency for Pharmaceuticals, Medical Consumables and Medical Equipment in East Africa for Multiple Multinational Companies who comply to the International Quality Standards as regulated by the GMP, WHO and ISO 9001:2015 Certified. In Kenya we are licensed and approved by the Pharmacy and Poisons Board (PPB).
